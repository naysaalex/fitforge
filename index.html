⌚ How to get your numbers from Apple Health
💡
Since your RingConn syncs to Apple Health, all your latest biometrics are in one place. Follow these steps, then fill in the fields below.
1
Open the Health app on your iPhone (white icon with a red heart).
2
Tap Summary (bottom left). Scroll down to see your recent highlights, or tap Browse (bottom right) to find specific categories.
3
Find each metric below. Tap it to see the most recent reading from your RingConn.
Where to find each metric
Weight
Browse → Body Measurements → Weight
Body Fat %
Browse → Body Measurements → Body Fat Percentage
Resting Heart Rate
Browse → Heart → Resting Heart Rate
HRV
Browse → Heart → Heart Rate Variability
SpO2 (Blood Oxygen)
Browse → Respiratory → Blood Oxygen
Sleep (hrs/night)
Browse → Sleep → Sleep Duration
Cycle Phase
Browse → Cycle Tracking → Cycle phase or RingConn app → Cycle tab
Waist (cm)
Measure manually with a tape — not in Apple Health
⚡
Pro tip: Screenshot your Apple Health Summary screen first so all numbers are visible while you fill in the form below.

Set your API key

FitForge uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate your personalized plans. Your key is stored only in your browser — it never leaves your device.

  1. Go to console.anthropic.com and sign up / log in
  2. Click API Keys in the left sidebar
  3. Click Create Key, give it a name, and copy it
  4. Paste it below and tap Save

💡 The free tier gives plenty of usage for personal plan generation (a few plans per month).
